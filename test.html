Deltascape v5.0 Alpha Version

The Story

DeltaScape v5.0 is a new dungeon in Final Fantasy XIV, created by Mik'ra, a member of the research & development team who is determined to uncover its secrets. As players explore the dungeon, they will encounter new challenges and puzzles that will test their skills and require them to work together to reach the end.

But as Mik'ra delves deeper into the mysteries of DeltaScape v5.0, he discovers something that he never expected. The information she has uncovered leads to a hidden HTML page that holds even more secrets and clues to the true nature of the dungeon. Can you help him unravel the mystery?

The Clues

As you explore this page and the world of DeltaScape v5.0, keep your eyes peeled for hidden clues that will lead you to the secret HTML page. The letters R, S, T, L, N, and E are your starting point, a reference to the Wheel of Fortune. Follow them to uncover the next step in the puzzle.
